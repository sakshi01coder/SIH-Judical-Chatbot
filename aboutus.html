Nyaya Bot, a virtual assistant for the Department of Justice, will provide users with essential judicial information and services. Key features include details on DoJ divisions, updates on judicial appointments, real-time case pendency via NJDG, guidance for traffic fine payments, eFiling, ePayment, and Fast Track Courts. It will also support live streaming of court cases, provide case status updates, and assist with the eCourts Services App and Tele Law Services. With its user-friendly interface, scalability, and adaptive learning, Nyaya Bot will be a comprehensive tool for accessing justice-related information.

Shri Arjun Ram Meghwal, Hon’ble Minister of State, Ministry of Law and Justice of India, is responsible for overseeing key aspects of the nation’s legal and judicial framework. His duties include formulating policies to strengthen the legal system, ensuring efficient judicial administration, and addressing issues related to judicial appointments and vacancies. He plays a crucial role in legislative reforms, promoting access to justice, and enhancing legal aid services. Additionally, he supports initiatives for modernizing court processes through technology, such as eCourts and digital legal services, aiming to make the justice system more accessible, transparent, and efficient for all citizens.

Hon’ble Chief Justice of India, Dr. Justice D.Y. Chandrachud, holds the highest judicial position in the country. As the head of the Supreme Court and the judiciary, his responsibilities include ensuring the smooth functioning of the courts, overseeing judicial appointments, and upholding the Constitution. He plays a pivotal role in shaping legal principles, setting judicial precedents, and safeguarding the rule of law. CJI Chandrachud also focuses on enhancing judicial efficiency through technological advancements and reforms, ensuring timely justice delivery, and promoting access to justice for all citizens.
